IntuitĂ­van: A spektrĂĄlfelbontĂĄs azt mondja, hogy minden szimmetrikus mĂĄtrix âegy forgatĂĄs + ĂĄtlĂł menti nyĂşjtĂĄs + visszaforgatĂĄs" â az ortogonĂĄlis $Q$ forog, a $\Lambda$ nyĂşjt.

OrtogonĂĄlis mĂĄtrix tulajdonsĂĄgai

$Q$ ortogonĂĄlis, ha $Q^TQ = QQ^T = I$, azaz $Q^{-1} = Q^T$. KĂśvetkezmĂŠnyek:

  • Az oszlopok egymĂĄsra merĹlegesek ĂŠs egysĂŠgnyiek: $\underline{q}_i^T\underline{q}_j = \delta_{ij}$.
  • $|Q| = \pm 1$ â ortogonĂĄlis mĂĄtrix nem ânyĂşjt", csak forgat/tĂźkrĂśz.
  • $\|Q\underline{x}\| = \|\underline{x}\|$ â megĹrzi a vektorok hosszĂĄt.

$$A\underline{x} = \sum_{k=1}^n \lambda_k\,(\underline{q}_k^T\underline{x})\,\underline{q}_k.$$
Olvasata: $\underline{q}_k^T\underline{x}$ az $\underline{x}$ vektor $k$-adik sajĂĄtirĂĄnyba esĹ komponense; $A$ ezt a komponenst $\lambda_k$-szorosĂĄra nyĂşjtja.

feltĂŠtel $A$-rasajĂĄtĂŠrtĂŠkek
$A$ szimmetrikusvalĂłs $\lambda_k$-k
$A$ pozitĂ­v szemidefinit (PSD)$\lambda_k \geq 0$ minden $k$-ra
$A$ pozitĂ­v definit (PD)$\lambda_k > 0$ minden $k$-ra

DeterminĂĄns ĂŠs nyom a sajĂĄtĂŠrtĂŠkekkel

$$|A| = \prod_{k=1}^n \lambda_k, \qquad \text{tr}(A) = \sum_{k=1}^n \lambda_k.$$
MiĂŠrt? $|A| = |Q\Lambda Q^T| = |Q||\Lambda||Q^T| = 1\cdot\prod\lambda_k\cdot 1$. Nyomra: $\text{tr}(Q\Lambda Q^T) = \text{tr}(\Lambda Q^TQ) = \text{tr}(\Lambda) = \sum\lambda_k$ (ciklikus tulajdonsĂĄg + $Q^TQ=I$).

KonkrĂŠt pĂŠlda â $2\times2$-es szimmetrikus mĂĄtrix

Legyen $A = \begin{pmatrix}3 & 1\\1 & 3\end{pmatrix}$. SajĂĄtĂŠrtĂŠkek: $\det(A-\lambda I) = 0$:

$$(3-\lambda)^2 - 1 = 0 \implies \lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 8 = 0 \implies \lambda_1 = 4,\; \lambda_2 = 2.$$

SajĂĄtvektorok ($A\underline{q} = \lambda\underline{q}$, majd normĂĄlĂĄs):

  • $\lambda_1 = 4$: $(A-4I)\underline{q}=0 \Rightarrow \begin{pmatrix}-1&1\\1&-1\end{pmatrix}\underline{q}=0 \Rightarrow \underline{q}_1 = \tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\begin{pmatrix}1\\1\end{pmatrix}$
  • $\lambda_2 = 2$: $(A-2I)\underline{q}=0 \Rightarrow \begin{pmatrix}1&1\\1&1\end{pmatrix}\underline{q}=0 \Rightarrow \underline{q}_2 = \tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\begin{pmatrix}1\\-1\end{pmatrix}$

VisszaellenĹrzĂŠs ($\lambda_k\underline{q}_k\underline{q}_k^T$ ĂśsszegzĂŠssel):

$$4\cdot\frac{1}{2}\begin{pmatrix}1&1\\1&1\end{pmatrix}+2\cdot\frac{1}{2}\begin{pmatrix}1&-1\\-1&1\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}2&2\\2&2\end{pmatrix}+\begin{pmatrix}1&-1\\-1&1\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}3&1\\1&3\end{pmatrix} = A. \checkmark$$

EllenĹrzĂŠs nyomra ĂŠs determinĂĄnsra: $\text{tr}(A) = 6 = 4+2$ â, $\;|A| = 8 = 4\cdot2$ â.

GyĂśk ĂŠs inverz spektrĂĄlfelbontĂĄssal

Ha $A = Q\Lambda Q^T$ PSD, akkor kĂśnnyen definiĂĄlhatĂł:

$$A^{1/2} = Q\Lambda^{1/2}Q^T, \quad\text{ahol}\quad \Lambda^{1/2} = \text{diag}(\sqrt{\lambda_1},\dots,\sqrt{\lambda_n}).$$ $$A^{-1} = Q\Lambda^{-1}Q^T, \quad\text{ahol}\quad \Lambda^{-1} = \text{diag}(1/\lambda_1,\dots,1/\lambda_n) \quad(\text{ha }\lambda_k>0).$$
Mikor lĂĄtjuk ezt? Wishart-mĂĄtrixoknĂĄl, Mahalanobis-tĂĄvolsĂĄgnĂĄl ($d^2 = (\underline{x}-\mu)^T\Sigma^{-1}(\underline{x}-\mu)$), ĂŠs a $\Sigma^{-1/2}$ fehĂŠrĂ­tĹ transzformĂĄciĂłnĂĄl, amivel standard Wishartra lehet visszavezetni.
